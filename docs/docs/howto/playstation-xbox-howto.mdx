# PlayStation & Xbox

Join a Minecraft server on your PS4, PS5 or Xbox.

## Before you start

Make sure:
- NetherLink is installed and running on your PC, Mac, or phone
- Your console and the device running NetherLink are on **the same Wi-Fi network**

---

## Steps

**1. Open NetherLink and go to Xbox / PS4-5 mode**

Enter the Minecraft server address and port you want to connect to.

**2. Press "Start Broadcasting"**

NetherLink will start broadcasting a fake LAN world on your network.

**3. On your console, open Minecraft**

Go to **Play → Friends tab** or look for **LAN Worlds**.

**4. You should see a world called "NetherLink"**

Select it and you're in!

---

## Not showing up?

A few things to try:

- Double check both devices are on the **same WiFi** mobile data won't work
- Make sure NetherLink is still broadcasting (don't close the app)
- Try toggling the broadcast off and back on
- Some routers block LAN discovery between devices if that's the case, check your router settings or try a different network

---

:::tip
On PS5 and newer Xbox models, LAN worlds sometimes take a few seconds to appear.
:::
