# Friends Mode Not Working

Friends mode can be a bit finicky — here's what to check if your friends can't see or join the session.

---

## Checklist

**1. Is the MCCompanion friend account added and accepted?**
On your console, make sure the MCCompanion account is in your friends list and the request is accepted not just pending.

**2. Have you refreshed the Friends/LAN tab?**
After enabling Friends mode, go into Minecraft and manually refresh the Friends or LAN Worlds tab. It won't always update on its own.

---

## Quick fixes

- **Restart the game** Close Minecraft completely, then reopen it and enable Friends mode again before loading in
- **Re-check the friend request** If the MCCompanion account was removed or the request expired, readd it and try again

---

## Still not showing up?

Enable **Debug Mode** in the MCCompanion app and check the logs. It'll usually tell you exactly what's failing whether it's an account issue, a MCCompanion issue, or something else.
