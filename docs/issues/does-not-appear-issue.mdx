# NetherLink Not Appearing

Your console isn't seeing the NetherLink LAN world? Work through these steps.

---

## Basic checks

Before anything else, confirm these three things:

- **Same Wi-Fi**  Your phone/PC and your console must be on the exact same network. Guest networks won't work
- **Correct address**  Double-check the server IP and port (default: `19132`)
- **Broadcasting is active**  The app should show a "Broadcasting" status, not idle or stopped

---

## Quick fixes

**Restart the app**
Stop broadcasting, fully close NetherLink, reopen it and try again.

**Restart your console**
Sometimes the console just needs a refresh to pick up new LAN games.

**Check the right tab**
The server shows up under **Friends** or **LAN Games** in Minecraft not in the server list.

**Wait a moment**
Give it 10 to 15 seconds after starting the broadcast before refreshing.

**Disable any VPN**
VPNs interfere with local network broadcasts. Turn it off while using NetherLink.

---

## Common error messages

**"NetherLink: No active session found!"**
You can fix this issue simply by Starting any mode, Our servers just need to know where to send you or you are not on the same network when starting NetherLink

**"Unable to connect to NetherLink server"**
This is an internet connectivity issue, not a LAN issue. Check your connection or see if the NetherLink is down.

---

## Still stuck?

Enable **Debug Mode** in the NetherLink app and check the logs it usually points directly to the problem. You can also try connecting to a different server to rule out a server side issue.
